Job Title - Web Developer

Job Summary

As a Web Developer at Hot Beans, you will be significant in creating user-friendly, responsive websites and web applications. You will work together with our team of designers and project managers to bring innovative ideas to life and provide our clients with outstanding solutions.

Key responsibilities

-Utilize modern programming languages and frameworks to develop and maintain responsive websites and web applications.
-Collaborate with designers to translate their creative concepts into functional, visually appealing website layouts.
-Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code while adhering to best practices and industry standards.

Requirements
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
Proven experience as a web developer or in an equivalent role, with a significant number of web projects.
Proficiency in front-end technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, or Vue.js).
Solid understanding of back-end development, including server-side languages such as Python.
