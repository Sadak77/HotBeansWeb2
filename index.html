About Us!

Hot Beans offers a fantastic opportunity for young individuals to explore the world of coding and embark on a journey to become skilled web developers. Our website not only provides resources and guidance for those who are already familiar with coding but also offers free online courses for youngsters who have no prior knowledge in this field. Starting from scratch, these courses aim to introduce coding concepts and ignite a passion for programming. By diving into the world of coding, they have the potential to develop their skills and eventually become professional web developers for our company in the future.
